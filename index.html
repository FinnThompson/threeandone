About The Band

Your Night, Our Sound

Three and One is a high-energy 4-piece cover band based out of the Jersey Shore and Philadelphia area. We bring the party to every stage — from intimate bar gigs to weddings, corporate events, and outdoor festivals.

Our setlist spans decades of crowd favorites — from classic rock anthems and blues grooves to modern pop and party hits. We don't just play songs; we create an experience that gets people out of their seats and onto the dance floor.
