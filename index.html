About The Band

Your Night, Our Sound

Three and One is a 4-piece cover band out of the Jersey Shore and Philly area. We play everything from bar gigs to weddings, corporate events, and outdoor festivals.

Our setlist covers decades of hits, from classic rock and blues to modern pop and party anthems. We're all about getting people out of their seats and onto the dance floor.

Meet The Band

Four players, one sound. Get to know who's on stage.

Gus

Lead Vocals / Guitar

Gus is a multi-instrumentalist whose job in the band is lead singer and guitarist. He’s been playing since he was just 6 years old and writes his own music. When he’s not gigging with the band he’s teaching people of all ages. As the eldest of the 3 brothers he’s always pretending to be more mature than the rest. He isn’t.

Finn

Keys / Bass / Backing Vocals

Finn covers keys, bass, and background vocals. He’s basically the band’s multitool. He’s been playing since he was 8, which explains why he looks suspiciously comfortable on stage. When he’s not writing tasty licks, he’s writing code (just pressing a different kind of keys).

Cosmo

Bass / Guitar / Backing Vocals

Cosmo holds down the low end in the band and is currently studying electrical engineering, so he knows a thing or two about good vibrations. As the youngest of the three brothers, he brings a lot of energy to the group and has earned the title of resident pinball wizard when he’s not onstage.

Neil

Drums

Neil is the drummer of the group. He’s been playing since he was a kid and has been keeping time — or some loose interpretation of it — ever since. When he’s not gigging with the band he’s taking photos, making ice cream at Springer’s, or serving with the NJ Air National Guard.
